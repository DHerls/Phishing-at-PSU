What is phishing?

Phishing is the act of creating a fake website, email, or other ruse in order to trick you into disclosing your password or other sensitive information to criminals.

Who am I and why am I doing this?

My name is Dan Herlihy and I am a freshman who will hopefully double major in Computer Science and Security & Risk Analysis. This website was created as part of an advocacy project for CAS 138T. I had the idea to create a project like this when I was watching the UPUA elections and watching people sign into their accounts on random people's computers. I realized how easy it would be for someone to steal accounts in that manner and so I decided to dedicate my advocacy project to raising awareness of phishing and identity theft. My professor in this class is Ben Henderson in the CAS department, and I personally can be reached for comments and questions at phishingawareness@psu.edu.
